Simple Ways To Help Reduce Land Pollution



  • Reusing materials help to reduce the requirement of harvesting resources. The products that cannot be reused can likely be recycled.
  • Reduce the use of pesticides and fertilizers in agricultural activities
  • Reduce the use of non-biodegradable materials. By simply switching to a reusable cloth bag for groceries instead of plastic shopping bags will help cut down on the need for non-biodegradable materials
  • Avoid buying packaged items as they will lead to creating garbage and end up in the landfill site.
  • Avoid littering and properly dispose of waste, which is an essential measure against land pollution both for households as well as for industries
  • Create dumping ground away from residential areas.
